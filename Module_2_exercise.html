Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Why choose Tom Yum Goong?

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp.

Before the ingredients, here's the real reason for prepping this savoury dish.

"The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors â€“ sweet, sour, salty, and spicy."

Ingredients:

  • Lemongrass: 2 stalks
  • Thai basil: 1 cup
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves: 3 leaves
  • Shrimp: 500g

Preparation:

  1. Simmerthe broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Seasonwith fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor.
  • 1tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat.
